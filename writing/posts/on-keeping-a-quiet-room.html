On keeping a quiet room

Most of the internet is loud. I wanted somewhere that wasn't.

This is the first thing I've written here, so it's really a note to myself as much as anyone. I built this room to have a place that moves at my pace — no feed, no metrics, no one refreshing to see if I've said something clever. Just a door, a little drifting light, and room to think.

A quiet room is not empty. It's full of everything you finally have space to hear.

I don't know exactly what will end up here. Some writing, surely. The projects I keep half-finishing. Maybe the small observations that never fit anywhere else. I'd rather let it grow slowly than decide it all at once.

If you've found your way in, thank you for being quiet with me for a moment. Stay as long as you like.
